Innovation Competition - 2019 (IC-19)

IUBAT Innovation and Entrepreneurship Center organized “Innovation Competition 2019” on 17 - th October 2019 at IUBAT Auditorium. Honorable Vice Chancellor of IUBAT Prof. Dr. Abdur Rab opened the ceremony. Distinguish external judges from BUET Prof. Dr. M. Kaykobad, Dr. Mohammad Jellur Rahman, Dr. Jubayer-Al-Mahmud, Chairman of dept. of CSE Prof Dr Md Abdul Haque, Dean of CBA Prof. Dr. Khair Jahan Sogra and the focal person of IUBAT Innovation Hub Dr. Hasibur Rashid Chayon were present at the event.

40 different teams from all departments of IUBAT participated in this event. 40 teams from all department participated in this event in two category-Poster presentation for non-engineering students and Project demonstration for engineering students. ‘Virtual Reality Simulation’ by Rubayet Ahmed & Mostafizur Rahman from BSME was Champion, ‘Robotic arm’ by Gopal Mondol & Abdullah al Nakib from BSME was the first runner-up and ‘Smart Injection’ by Sheekar Banerjee & Saleh bin Sayeem from BCSE was the second runner-up of this event. Shamima Khandaker Aishi from BECon won the best female Inventor for her ‘Green Banking’ project. Students, Faculties and staffs from all departments enjoyed this first Innovation Competition of IUBAT. Honorable Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Abdur Rab concluded the event in the prize giving ceremony at open Auditorium.
