About the competition

The Techno CAD segment of Mindsparks-23 aims to promote the field of Computer-Aided Design (CAD) among college, and university students in Bangladesh. The competition encourages participants to develop their skills in CAD and apply their knowledge to practical projects. The goal is to improve the quality of education in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) and to create awareness of the importance of CAD in various industries. The Techno CAD segment also aims to foster an entrepreneurial spirit among participants, helping them to think creatively and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Eligible Participant Education Levels for the Competition:

  • - The competition is open to all students of College and undergraduate university students.
  • - Participants must participate as a team of not more than 03 members. Solo members will also be counted as a team.
  • - Cross-institution teams are allowed.
  • - A person will not be allowed to register for two different teams.
