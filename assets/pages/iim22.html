IIIM - IUBAT Innovative Idea Manifest (2022)

The IUBAT Innovation Idea Manifest competition is an exciting event that aims to promote innovation and creativity among students from different departments of IUBAT. The competition is open to all students, and its primary objective is to present innovative projects and ideas. Participants are given two categories of predefined ideas to choose from while showcasing their projects. This requirement ensures that the ideas presented by the students are relevant and practical. The categories could be anything, from environmental sustainability to the latest technological advancements.

The projects are evaluated by a panel of selected honorable judges who assess the quality, feasibility, and potential impact of the presented ideas. This ensures that the winning ideas are not only innovative but also have practical applications in the real world. The winners of the competition are awarded prize money to further develop or improve their projects under the guidance and supervision of the IUBAT Innovation & Entrepreneurship Center. This support encourages students to continue pursuing their innovative ideas and transform them into tangible products or services that benefit society.
