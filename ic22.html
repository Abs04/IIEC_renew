Innovation Competition - 2022 (IC-22)

The Innovation Competition 2022 was organized by the IUBAT Innovation and Entrepreneurship Center (IIEC), with support from a2i, ICT Division, and Innovation Lab. The competition featured seven different segments, including the Ingenious Challenge, Automation & Industry 4.0, Soccer Bot, Bot Fight, Business Case Study, Article writing & poster presentation, and Economic & Computational Agriculture. The total prize money for the competition was 100K, which was to be awarded to the winners of each segment.

The Ingenious Challenge segment focused on encouraging innovative solutions to real-world problems. The Automation & Industry 4.0 segment aimed to promote the use of advanced technologies such as robotics and automation in the manufacturing industry. The Soccer Bot and Bot Fight segments challenged participants to design and build robots capable of playing soccer or engaging in battle, respectively. The Business Case Study segment called for submissions of comprehensive analyses of real-world business cases, while the Article writing & poster presentation segment was designed to showcase research on innovative topics related to technology and entrepreneurship. Finally, the Economic & Computational Agriculture segment aimed to promote the use of technology in agriculture to enhance efficiency and productivity.
